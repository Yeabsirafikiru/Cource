MESOB-One-Stop Digital Service Center

MESOB is a citizen-centered digital service platform launched by the Government of ethiopia to streamline access to public services.

  • Key Features

    • Unified Access: Citizens can access multiple service from one portal.
    • Transparency: Real-time tracking of service requests.
    • Efficiency: Reduce paperwork and faster processing.
  • Service Offered

    1. Business License Renewal
    2. Land Registration
    3. Taxpayer Identyfication Number (TIN) Issuance
    4. Vital Recoreds (Birth, Marriage, Death Certificates)
  • Key Features

  • All services are available in both Amharic and English.
  • For technical support, contact mesob-support@gov.et or dial 6090.
  • Definition

    e-Service
    Is any government service delivered electronically via the internet.
    Digital money
    Is a unique identifier used to authenticate citizens online.
